# Notes:
"In Goizueta both the position of the stress and the tonal contour or pitch-accent associated with the stressed syllable are lexically
contrastive." p123 Diacritic  ́  is a rise over the stressed vowel (a high tone), and diacritic  ̀  is falling contour (a low tone).  Above, these are presented as allophones of a single phoneme.
