# Notes:
Minimal pair examples from JIPA paper.
Geminate nasals and long vowels are written with a double graph in JIPA paper. Here the length symbol has been used for 
consistency across languages. 
"Ibibio is a register terraced level tone language with two level tones (H)igh and (L)ow. Combinations" p107 JIPA
of the level tones from tone spreading and segment deletion result in the formation of two types of contour tones, namely, 
High-Low and Low-High, which are not contrastive with each other." Also a downstep.
