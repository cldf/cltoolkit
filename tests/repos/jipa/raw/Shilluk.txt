# Notes:
There is a three way distinction between length in vowels, which in the paper is written 
as single letter for short, double letter for long and triple letter for overlong.  
Here I have used single symbol for short, the half-long symbol for long and the 
long symbol for overlong. 
Geminate consonants are indicated in the paper with a doubling of the letter.  Here I have 
used the length symbol.  
Tone: Low (cv́c), Mid (cv̄c), High (cv̀c), Rise (cv̌c), Fall (cv̂c), High Fall (cv̂́c), 
and Late Fall (cv́c̀).  Each possible tone type is expressed on vowels which are presented 
as allophones.  Consonants that take the late fall in the transcription are expressed as
allophones of the consonant phoneme. 
The transcription is phonemic.
