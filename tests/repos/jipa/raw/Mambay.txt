# Notes:
/ɗ/ is an implosive retroflex. /ⱱ̟/ is a bilabial flap.

Glottal stop is identified as a morpheme boundary marker by the JIPA authors, but not posited as a phoneme. 
Here I have included it in the phoneme inventory, as we have no other way of representing it.

High tone [á]; low tone [à]; High-low tone [â]; downstep ↓
