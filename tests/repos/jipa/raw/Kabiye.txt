# Notes:
 The orthography is discussed in the paper.
 
In the JIPA article the vowels /ɪ, ʊ, a, ɛ, ɔ, ɪː, ʊː, aː, ɛː, ɔː, ɪˠ, aˠ, ɛˠ/ 
all consistently took the retracted tongue root diacritic, but I was unable to 
recreate it here.

There are two tones marks: low <à> and high <í>, and downsteps.
