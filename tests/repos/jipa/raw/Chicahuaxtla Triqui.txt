# Notes:
The broad transcription has been used here. There is also a narrow transcription
in the JIPA paper.
Chicahuaxtla Triqui has 5 basic tones and 10-15 tonal contours. In the illustration tones are expressed by 
superscript numbers 1-5, with 1 being the lowest and 5 the highest.
